ORDER

Heard the learned counsel for the _ petitioner and
respondents.

2. Petitioner is the accused No.1 in Crime No. 78/2007 of
Cumbummettu Police Station, Idukki District pending as L.P.No.
6/2012 on the file of the Sessions Court, Thoudpuzha. The
petitioner is charge sheeted for the offence punishable under
Sections 8 (1) and (2) of the Abkari Act and under Section 3 (1)
of PDPP Act. Due to the non-appearance of the petitioner, non-
bailable warrant was issued against him.

3. The petitioner is willing to surrender before the court
below. Non-bailable warrant is pending against him. Now, the
petitioner seeks a direction from this Court to consider his
application for bail on the date of surrender itself.

In the facts and circumstances, the Crl.M.C is disposed of,
directing the Sessions Court, Thodupuzha to consider and pass
orders on the bail application on the date of surrender itself in the

event of the petitioner surrendering before the court below and
