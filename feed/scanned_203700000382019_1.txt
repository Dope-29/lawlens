FOLLOWING:

ORDER

Petition filed under Section 482 Cr.P.C.

2. The petitioners are the accused in Crime No.
843 of 2018 of Pavaratty Police Station
registered for the offences under Sections
323, 324 & 341 of the Indian Penal Code.
Respondents 2 & 3 are the victims. It is
submitted that the matter has been settled and
the proceedings in the criminal case may be
quashed.

3. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners
and respondents 2 & 3 and the learned Public
Prosecutor.

4. I have perused the affidavits filed by
