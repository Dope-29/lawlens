ORDER

Petitioner claims to be the registered owner of the vehicle
bearing registration No.KL-10-S-6131. The said vehicle was
seized by the respondent in connection with a crime of Areacode
Police Station on the allegation of illegal transportation of river
sand.

2. The petitioner filed CMP No.6408/2013 before the Judicial
First Class Magistrate Court-I, Manjeri seeking interim custody of
the vehicle. The court ordered interim custody subject to eight
conditions. The court ordered that the petitioner shall deposit
Rs.33,000/-  and_ shall also furnish bank guarantee for
Rs.77,000/-. He is also directed to execute a bond for Rs.
1,10,000/- with two solvent sureties each for the like sum.

3. The petitioner is aggrieved by the condition to deposit Rs.
33,000/- and to furnish bank guarantee for Rs.77,000/- as
security. It is submitted that he is unable to comply with the
above conditions and that he is willing to execute a bond for

Rs.1,10,000/- with two solvent sureties each for the like sum.

4. Having regard to the facts and circumstance of the

The Crl.M.C is disposed of as above.
