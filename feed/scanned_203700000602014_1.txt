ORDER

Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners and respondents.

2. Accused in Crime No.2/2009 of Kottayi Police Station pending
as C.C.No.636/2009 on the file of the Judicial First Class Magistrate
Court III, Palakkad are the petitioners. The petition is filed seeking to
quash further proceedings against the petitioners in Annexure-A Final
Report in Crime No. 2/2009 of Kottayi Police Station.

3. The offences alleged are punishable under Sections 498 A r/w
Section 34 of IPC.

4. The learned counsel on both sides submit that the disputes
between the parties have been amicably settled and having regard to
the resolution of the disputes between the parties, continuance of the
criminal prosecution against the petitioners is an avoidable irritant.

In view of the settlement arrived at between the parties, the
Cri.M.C is disposed of quashing further proceedings against the
petitioners in Annexure-A Final Report in Crime No. 2/2009 of Kottayi

Police Station.
