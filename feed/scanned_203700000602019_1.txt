The petitioner has filed this
Cr1.M.C. seeking for modification of
condition No.2 in Annexure-Al order dated

17.01.2018.
2. Heard the learned counsel for the
petitioner and the learned Public Prosecutor.
3. It was clearly stated in
condition No.2 in Annexure-Al order that if
any modification was required, the trial
court would be at liberty to pass
appropriate orders at the appropriate stage.
Therefore, the petitioner filed Annexure-A2
application before the court below seeking
for modification of condition No.2 in
Annexure-Al order. However, the petitioner

did not press for hearing that petition, as

is evident from Annexure-A3. Now, the
petitioner has approached this Court praying
for modification of condition No.2 in
Annexure-Al order.

4. In view of the clear direction in
condition No.2 in Annexure-Al order, the
petitioner must approach the court below to
redress his grievance before approaching
this Court. If the petitioner files any
such petition for modification of condition
No.2 in Annexure-Al order, the court below
shall consider and dispose of the said
petition, in accordance with law, as
expeditiously as possible, and at any rate,
within one month from the date of filing of

the petition.
