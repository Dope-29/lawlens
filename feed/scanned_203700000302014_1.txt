ORDER

Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners and
respondents.

2. Accused Nos. 1, 3 and 4 in Crime No. 286 of 2010 of
Koduvally Police Station, pending as C.C. No.537 of 2010 before
the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court-I, Thamarasserry are the
petitioners. The petition is filed seeking to quash further
proceedings against the petitioners in C.C. No.537 of 2010 before
the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court-I, Thamarasserry.

3. The offences alleged are punishable under Sections 406
and 498 A IPC.

4. The learned counsel on both sides submit that the
disputes between the parties have been amicably settled and having
regard to the resolution of the disputes between the parties,
continuance of the criminal prosecution against the petitioners is an
avoidable irritant.

In view of the settlement arrived at between the parties, the
Crl.M.C is disposed of quashing all further proceedings in C.C.
No.537 of 2010 before the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court-I,
Thamarasserry.
