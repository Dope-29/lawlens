ORDER

Annexure Ai is the copy of the order dated 6.6.2013 in
Crl.M.P.No.868/2013 on the file of the Judicial First Class
Magistrate Court, Malappuram. Petitioner seeks modification of
the said order. The vehicle owned by the petitioner was seized by
the Station House Officer, Vengara on the allegation of illegal
transportation of river sand. Learned Magistrate ordered release
of the vehicle subject to seven conditions. Condition Nos.1,2 & 3
reads as follows:

(i) Petitioner shall execute a bond for Rs.30,000/- with two
solvent sureties each for the like sum.

(ii) Petitioner shall deposit Rs.10,000/- in the court.

(iii) Petitioner shall produce security or bank guarantee for
Rs.20,000/-.

2. It is submitted that the petitioner is not in a position to
arrange sureties as nobody will stand as surety for the release of
the vehicle. It is also submitted that the petitioner is not having
any landed property in his name and is unable to arrange bank

guarantee for the amount. Petitioner is ready to deposit entire

3. In the facts and circumstances, condition Nos.1 to 3 are

The Crl.M.C is disposed of as above.
